** Role of UI/UX Design in Application Development **

   1. Improves User Experience
      -> Good UI/UX design makes the application easy to use, intuitive, and enjoyable, reducing user frustration.

   2. Enhances Usability
      -> Clear layouts, logical navigation, and consistent design help users complete tasks quickly and accurately.

   3. Increases User Engagement
      -> Visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces encourage users to spend more time on the app.

   4. Reduces Learning Time
      -> Simple and familiar design patterns help new users understand the application faster.

   5. Boosts Customer Satisfaction
      -> A positive user experience builds trust and increases user satisfaction and loyalty.

   6. Supports Business Goals
      -> Good UX improves conversion rates, retention, and overall success of the application.

   7. Reduces Development Cost
      -> Identifying usability issues early reduces rework and costly changes after development.
