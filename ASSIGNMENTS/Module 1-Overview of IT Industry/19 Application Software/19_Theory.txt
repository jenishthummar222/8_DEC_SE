** Role of Application Software in Businesses **

   -> Application software plays a crucial role in businesses by automating daily operations, 
      improving communication, managing data, and supporting decision-making. 

   -> It helps businesses handle accounting, customer relationships, inventory, human resources, and 
      project management efficiently. Application software increases productivity, reduces operational costs, 
      minimizes errors, and enables better customer service. 

   -> It also supports remote work, digital marketing, and business analytics, making organizations more 
      competitive and efficient in the modern digital world.
